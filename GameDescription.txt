# SunBlastDeflection
Short 
Html5, Javascript, CSS Graphics Programming Project



Simple Game:

** Start the game by pressing "Start Game" Button.
** Reset in game progress with "Reset Game" Button.

** Enter Player First Name to be included in your gameOver popup!
** Move the Deflecter with space bar to reverse your direction!
** Defend against Sun Blasts from the almighty star
** to keep planets health good!

** Dont let the Suns blasts Score get to 20 or game over! 
** Blasts will get more frequent!
